Insurance Eligibility Verification

Accurate, real-time insurance eligibility verification to prevent denials, reduce patient balance surprises, and ensure services are reimbursed correctly before care is delivered.

Why Eligibility Verification Is Critical

Eligibility errors remain one of the leading causes of claim denials in the United States. Verifying insurance coverage prior to services protects providers from avoidable write-offs and improves patient financial transparency.

Industry Research:
According to CAQH and MGMA data, over 25% of claim denials are directly linked to eligibility and coverage verification errors.

Our Verification Process

Real-Time Eligibility Checks

We verify coverage directly through payer portals, clearinghouses, and real-time eligibility systems prior to the date of service.

Benefits & Limitations Review

Coverage details include active policy status, deductible balances, co-payments, co-insurance, exclusions, and benefit limitations.

Authorization Requirements

We identify prior authorization and referral requirements to prevent medical necessity and technical denials.

Patient Responsibility Estimation

Estimated patient responsibility is calculated to support transparent financial discussions before treatment.
