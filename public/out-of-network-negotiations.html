Our negotiation process

A step-by-step workflow proven across specialties (internal medicine, surgery, psychiatry, dental).

1. Data & claim audit

We analyze payer denials, allowed amounts, and historical reimbursement by CPT/DRG.

2. Build evidence file

Documented medical necessity, appropriate CPT mapping, and supporting chart notes prepared for negotiation.

3. Payer engagement

We open structured negotiation channels with payer reps and present data-backed requests.

4. Agreement & tracking

We secure a written agreement and monitor future claims to ensure payer compliance.
