ABOUT US

Physicians Claim Partners is a specialized medical & dental revenue recovery team helping practices reclaim lost revenue. We handle insurance claim submission, denials follow-up, patient billing, and payer verification — so your practice’s financial side runs without you having to manage it.

We work directly inside your existing practice-management software (Dentrix, Eaglesoft, OpenDental and others) and act as an extension of your team — no replacing staff, no disruption to workflow, and no learning curve for your front desk.

Our clients range from solo physicians to multi-site groups. The common thread is the same — money sitting in unpaid claims starts moving, collections stabilize, and your staff gets their time back.

WHO WE WORK WITH

Dental billing problems don’t look the same in every practice, but they usually come from the same place – too much work, not enough time, and insurance rules that change faster than anyone can keep up with.

We work with solo dentists and small private practices where the front desk is doing everything at once and billing keeps getting pushed to the end of the day. We work with specialty offices — periodontists, oral surgeons, orthodontists, endodontists, pediatric dentists — where coding is more complex and a single missed detail on a claim means a significant payment delayed or denied.

We work with practices that are growing — adding chairs, opening a second location, taking on more patients — and need billing to scale without hiring more staff. We work with start-ups that want clean operations from day one and don’t want to figure out insurance credentialing and claims submission on their own.

And we work with practices that are already behind. Aging AR, a backlog of denied claims, a biller who just quit, collections stuck at 60–70% for months — if that’s where you are right now, that’s exactly the situation we’re used to walking into.

If insurance billing is slowing your practice down, we can help — regardless of your size, software, or how complicated things have gotten.

WHY PRACTICES COME TO US

CASH FLOW THAT NEVER QUITE CATCHES UP

You're seeing patients, the work is getting done, but the money isn't landing when it should. Claims sit in queues, insurers take their time, and by the end of the month the numbers don't match what you produced. This is almost never a patient volume problem. It's a billing process problem — and it's fixable.

DENIALS THAT KEEP COMING BACK

A denied claim isn't just lost revenue. It's someone's time to investigate it, correct it, resubmit it, and wait again. When denials become a pattern the real cost is the hours your team spends firefighting instead of working the front desk. We submit clean claims the first time and follow up on every single one that doesn't pay.

AR THAT KEEPS AGING

Most practices have money sitting in 90-, 120-, even 180-day AR that nobody has had time to chase. It's not gone — it's just stuck. Cleaning up old AR is one of the first things we do when we start with a new practice, and for most offices it's where the fastest visible results come from.

A FRONT DESK DOING TOO MANY JOBS AT ONCE

Scheduling, check-ins, patient questions, treatment plans, phones — and somewhere in between insurance verification and claim submission. When your front desk is stretched, revenue feels it first. We let your front desk focus on patients while we handle the billing workflows.

WE SCALE WITH YOU

Whether you're a solo dentist, a growing multi-chair practice, or a specialty office — we provide processes and staffing to match your volume so billing doesn't become a bottleneck as you grow.

HOW IT WORKS

Simple to start. Straightforward to run

One of the most common concerns we hear is that switching to outsourced billing will be complicated – that it will disrupt the front desk, create a messy handover, or take weeks before anything improves. In practice it works the other way. Most offices are fully up and running within a few days, and the team usually notices the difference within the first billing cycle. Here is what the process looks like.

1

We talk about your practice

You tell us where things stand – what software you use, where the biggest billing problems are, and what you need from us. No lengthy forms, no sales pressure. Just a straightforward conversation so we understand your situation before we start.

2

We get access and get to work

We set up access to your practice management software and establish a clear workflow with your team. We define who handles what, how we communicate, and what you can expect to see from us each week. Then we start working your claims and AR from day one.

3

Your billing runs, you see the results

Claims go out clean, follow-up happens on schedule, and your AR starts moving. You get regular updates on what’s been submitted, what’s been collected, and what’s still outstanding. No chasing us for information – you always know where things stand.

Most practices are surprised by how little they have to manage once we’re set up. That’s the point.
