AR & Denial Management Services

We specialize in aggressive accounts receivable and denial management to recover lost revenue, reduce aging, and protect the financial health of medical and dental practices across the United States.

Accounts receivable and claim denials are among the biggest causes of revenue loss for healthcare practices. Unpaid claims, underpayments, and unresolved denials can quickly accumulate into significant financial leakage if not addressed systematically and aggressively.

At Physicians Claim Partners, our AR & Denial Management team focuses on identifying root causes, correcting workflow gaps, and ensuring every unpaid or underpaid claim is followed through until resolution.

Our structured approach not only recovers outstanding revenue but also prevents future denials by improving front-end processes, documentation accuracy, and payer-specific compliance.

Accounts Receivable Follow-Up

Systematic follow-up on outstanding claims across 30, 60, and 90+ day aging buckets to ensure timely reimbursement and reduced AR days.

Denial Analysis & Appeals

Detailed denial analysis, correction of claim errors, and submission of payer-specific appeals with supporting documentation.

Underpayment Identification

Verification of payments against contracted fee schedules to identify and recover underpaid claims.

Root Cause Prevention

Identification of recurring denial trends and implementation of corrective measures to prevent future revenue loss.
