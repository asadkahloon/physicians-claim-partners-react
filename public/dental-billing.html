Dental Billing Services (USA)

We provide specialized dental billing services designed to maximize collections, reduce write-offs, and ensure accurate insurance reimbursement for dental practices across the United States.

Dental billing is significantly different from medical billing. Incorrect CDT coding, missing attachments, unverified fee schedules, and poor insurance follow-ups can lead to substantial revenue loss for dental practices.

At Physicians Claim Partners, our dental billing specialists focus exclusively on dental claims. Every claim is verified before submission, every EOB is reconciled line by line, and unpaid claims are followed aggressively until resolution.

Our clients experience improved collections, reduced aging, fewer denials, and complete visibility into their dental revenue cycle—allowing dentists to focus on patient care.

CDT Coding & Clean Claims

Accurate CDT coding, documentation verification, and clean claim submission to minimize denials and delays.

EOB Reconciliation

Detailed review of insurance EOBs against contracted fee schedules to identify underpayments and missed reimbursements.

PPO Fee Schedule Optimization

Analysis of PPO contracts to ensure correct payments and recovery of underpaid procedures.

Aging & AR Follow-Up

Dedicated follow-ups on 30/60/90-day aging claims, appeals, and patient balance resolution.
